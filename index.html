Digital Composition

Bioshock Fan Art

If it's one thing you need to know about me after visiting my website, it's that I enjoy video games. This work was inspired by the action-adventure, horror themed first person shooter, Bioshock. Because this game is set entirely underwater, I wanted to keep that theme and choose the color scheme accordingly. I decided to showcase Big Daddies and Little Sisters because their use of duality between the two always stuck with me.


Glitched Art

Found Phone

With this design I used Audacity to glitch a photo of a phone homescreen to give off an ominous feeling. What inspired me to do that was this, "found phone" horror gamed called Sumulacra where you find a missing girls phone that's corrupted and you go through her phone to find clues about what happened and where she is but you quickly discover her disappearance is supernatural.

Meshmixer Design

Infected Man

This lovely looking man was inspired by the clicker zombies in The Last of Us, an action-adventure video game. Instead of a sporadtic fungus infection, I decided I would use coral because it is something different all the while still looking just as parasitic.
